POS - Billing

  • ‘POS’ (Point Of Sale) - A point-of-sale system, or POS, is the place where a customer makes a payment for products or services at a store. Simply put, every time a customer makes a purchase, they’re completing a point-of-sale transaction. The main purpose of this feature is to facilitate the process of retail billing operations in Unify. 
  • This functionality will be available under the POS menu and based on the user rights given, the users/cashiers will be able to access this functionality. Given that the cashier has access to multiple stores of multiple companies then on clicking the POS menu a popup window will be displayed to select the desired company.

COMPANY SELECTION:

 

  • The company selection window will display the companies assigned to the cashier with the company name and company logo. If the cashier has access to only one company then on clicking the POS menu, the cashier will be redirected to the store selection window.

     

STORE SELECTION

 

  • On selecting the company the stores belonging to the selected company will be displayed where the cashier can select the store to be logged in.

     
  • Search Box:  Cashiers can also search for the available stores by store code or store name using the search box.



 

COUNTER SELECTION

 

  • On selecting the store, the counters belonging to the selected store will be displayed where the cashier can select the counter to be logged in. 

     
  • Toggle and Search Box: Cashier can also toggle between available and logged-in counters and can also search the counters by counter code or counter name using the search box available on the top right of the window.

     
  • On the counter selection window. Each card will have the counter number, counter code, and counter name as identification to select the desired counter for the cashier.



 

  • On selecting a counter, the cashier will be redirected to the shift begin page where the cashier will be entering the denominations of the opening cash received to start the counter. 

     
  • In case the store is located at an airport where foreign currency is allowed then the cashier has the ability to enter the denominations in the other currencies using the currency dropdown available on the top left.

     
  • On clicking begin a shift, the cashier will be redirected to the billing screen and the selected counter will now be in running status.

     
  • In the billing screen by default billing mode will be selected and the salesperson 1 belonging to the logged-in store will be displayed which was configured in the retail configuration template of that store.
  • CUSTOMER SELECTION:
  • By default a walk-in customer will be selected which is configured in the retail configuration template and the fields to be captured for a walkin customer will be displayed as per the custom fields configured in the template for the walk-in customer template.

     
  • On clicking on the customer search field, a dropdown will be displayed with multiple walkin customers where the cashier can select different walkin customers to get the fields configured in that walkin customer.
  • The cashier can also search for an existing registered customer by clicking on the cross icon available in the customer search field and can search the customers using First Name, Last Name, Email Id, and Mobile Number.
  • On searching, the customers will be displayed in the dropdown and the cashier can select the registered customer from the dropdown.
  • On selecting the customer, the customer's first and last name will be displayed along with the customer's image. The cashier will also have the option to remove the selected customer or edit the customer details by clicking on the manage button.
  • CUSTOMER CREATION
  • A new customer can be created by clicking on the Create new (+) button available in the drop-down of the customer search field. On clicking Create new customer the fields configured in the default customer template will be displayed here for quick customer creation in which the cashier can enter the details of the customer and click on save.

     
  • On clicking save an OTP popup will be displayed on the screen where the cashier can enter the OTP received by the customer - this can be optional based on the configuration of the default customer template.
  • OTP can be sent to both Email ID and mobile number and the OTP consists of 4 digits with a timer of 5 minutes after which a new OTP can be generated using the resend button.

     
  • Customers can enter the received OTP on the dual-display screen which will be captured on the billing screen.

     
  • The cashier can also click on the change number button in case the mobile number was entered incorrectly and the new mobile number can be entered by the customer in dual display or the cashier can enter the new number on the billing screen.

     
  • In case the entered OTP is incorrect then the OTP verification failed message will be displayed on the OTP window and the cashier can re enter the OTP.

     
  • If the OTP verification on registration in the customer template which is attached to the store is configured as optional then the ‘Save Without Verification’ button will be available on the OTP window.
  • On clicking the ‘Save Without Verification’ button, the new customer will be created without entering the OTP and the created customer will be shown on the left panel of the screen.
  • Apart from the quick customer creation, additional information related to the customer to be created can be provided by clicking on the add more button which will open up the customer creation window.

     
  • In this window, the cashier can also upload an image of the customer or capture the image of the customer if a camera is connected to the POS system. The other fields to capture details will be available as per the customer template configured.
  • Quick Customer lookup:
  • The Cashiers can search for existing registered customers using quick customer lookup available on the customer search field. On clicking on the quick customer lookup button a popup window will appear with the searchable fields configured in the retail configuration template under lookup configuration type.
  • The cashier can search for the existing customers using the searchable fields and on clicking the apply button filtered customers will be displayed on the grid below.
  • The cashier can select a customer from the available customers and edit the registered customer by clicking on the pencil icon which will open up the customer edit popup window similar to the manage button.
  • The cashier can also create a new customer by clicking on the create new customer button available in the top right corner of the quick customer lookup window.
  • On clicking on the create new customer button, a customer creation popup window will open up where the cashier can enter all the required details to create a new customer.
  • SALESPERSONS:
  • The salesperson configured for the store in the retail configuration template will be selected by default in the primary salesperson and the first available salesperson in the list will be selected by default in the secondary salesperson.
  • The salespersons created in employee master for the particular stores will be available in the primary and secondary salespersons dropdowns.
  • The selected salespersons at the header level will also be visible at the line level for each product scanned and the cashier can change the salespersons based on the product sold by the salesperson to the customers.
  • The cashier can also search for the salespersons by entering the code or name of the salesperson in both header level sales persons and line level sales persons.
  • Product Search:
  • In the billing, the product search box will be used to scan the barcodes of the products for billing and search the products by entering the product name, product code, or alias code. 
  • Cashiers can enter the first four characters of the product name, product code, or alias code in order to search for the product, and on entering the characters system will display the relevant products based on the characters entered by the cashier.
  • The dropdown in the product search box displays the product image, product name, product code, and price of the product with the store currency code.
  • Multi Pricing:
  • On scanning a product, if there are multiple price lists created for that product then a popup window will be displayed where the cashier can select from the available prices.

     
  • The cashier can also sort the prices based on the expiry of the prices or prices from high to low or low to high using the sort drop-down available in the window.
  • Lot Controlled:
  • On scanning a product, if it is a lot-controlled (batch/serialized) product then a batch/serial number browser popup window will be displayed where the cashier can choose the lot number of the product to be sold to the customer.

     
  • In the batch/serial number browser, the cashier can filter the lot numbers using the available search criteria like From Lot Number, To lot number, Expiry Date, Best Before, Sort By, Supplier, Manufacturer, Sales Price.
  • Quick Product Lookup:
  • The cashiers can also search for the products using quick product lookup available on the product search field. On clicking on the quick product lookup button a popup window will appear with the searchable fields configured in the retail configuration template under lookup configuration type.
  • The cashier can search for the existing products using the searchable fields and on clicking the apply button filtered products with be displayed on the grid below. The cashier can select a product from the available products in the grid and on selecting the product will be added to the billing grid.

     
  • Cashiers will be able to find the available stock, sales price, brand, and category available in the popup window grid for the filtered products. The clear button can be used to clear the filter entered/selected in the lookup fields and the cashier can again reselect the filters to search available products.

 

  • Product Grid:
  • On scanning the products in the product search box, product details like product name, product image, quantity, RRP, sales price, discount, and net unit price amount will be displayed in the primary row of the product grid.
  • In the secondary row, additional information like charges, taxes, salesperson 1, salesperson 2, serial number, node, and brand will be displayed and the cashier can customize both primary and secondary rows of the product grid by using the settings icon.
  • Out of the configurable four fields in the primary row, cashier can enable any two fields and for secondary row cashier can enable any four fields available from the list of configurable fields.

     
  • The cashier can also reset to the default configured fields by clicking on the reset button available on the configuration window. On clicking on the reset button, the default values which were set by the system will be applied to the configuration.
  • Scan level Promotions:
  • %  or Amount Discount on Retail Price
  • In this promotion % or amount discount will be offered on the line amount of the scanned product
  • Offer Price Promotion
  •  In this promotion, the product can be offered at a lower price than MRP
  • Checkout:

    • After the products are added to the grid, the cashier can proceed to checkout by clicking on the checkout button available on the bottom right corner of the screen.

       
    • On clicking the checkout button, the payment modes which were configured for the store or counter in the retail configuration template will be displayed where the cashier can tender the payments received by the customer.
  • After the payments are accepted by the cashier, now the cashier can click on the submit button to initiate the saving of the invoice, on clicking submit button, the cashier can virtually verify the invoice which will be displayed on the screen and then the cashier can save the bill to generate the invoice for the customer.



 

  • Cashiers can accept multiple payments from the customer by using the multiple payment modes available which are configured in the retail configuration for the store or for a specific counter.
  • Payment modes like cash, debit/credit card, cheque, demand draft, Traveller's cheque, Gift Voucher, Credit Note, Bank Transfer, Foreign Currency, E-wallets, and other payment like coupons.
  • Checkout Promotions:
  • Buy and Get a discount on another product

In this promotion % or amount discount will be offered on the line amount of the specified product.


Example: Buy 1 and Get 50 % OFF on Another Product.
As its Inclusive promotion, If both the products are in the grid then on the Click on Checkout, Then 50% Line Discount will be applied to the product mentioned in action.

  • Free Product Promotion
  • In this promotion Free Product is Rewarded when purchased from a certain group of products. In this scenario, the action will be given free of cost.

     

Example:  Buy 1 Get 1 Using Free Product Action - As it is an Inclusive promotion both the products are to be scanned in grid and on Click of checkout, This promotion is to be shown under the Applied Promotion.

  • Value Buy Promotion
    • In this Promotion Combo of Products is valued at a discounted price and the combo discount amount is split among the promotional products.

       
    • Example: Value buy promotion - Combo of 3, If all three mentioned products are in the grid then on the click on Checkout, these products will be shown Under a combo in Applied promotion
  • % or Amount Discount on Bill Value:
  • In this promotion % or Amount discount is offered based on the total Bill value
  • Example: Shop Worth Rs.15000 and Get 12% OFF - If the bill amount is equal to or more than Rs.15000 then 12% Header Discount will be applied on the total amount

     
  • Coupon Promotion:
    • In this Promotion promo codes are offered to a group of customers on certain purchase conditions.
    • Example: Use UNIFY20 and get 20% off on shopping above 800/- Scan the coupon code in the promo code/voucher field, after adding products in the grid, on clicking checkout if the net amount is =800, to apply 20% discount on the Net Amount

       
  • Cash Payment Mode:

    • By default, the balance amount to be paid will be displayed in the received amount field in this payment mode. The cashier can modify the amount to be received in case the customer is willing to pay a partial amount of the bill with cash and the remaining amount of the bill with another payment mode.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid and the cashier will be able to delete the payments received in case the customer wants to pay through another payment mode.
  • Credit/Debit Card Payment Mode:

    • The cashier can opt for a credit/debit payment mode in case the customer wants to pay through a credit or debit card. Here, by default, the amount to be received or the balance amount will be shown on the received amount field, and the cashier can select the EDC device being used, and enter the first 6 and last 4 digits of the credit/debit card given by the customer. 
    • On entering the first 6 digits of the card, the system will automatically fetch the details of the card like Card brand, Card Type, and Bank and the cashier can enter the cardholder name, expiry date of the card, and the approval number received on processing the payment.



 

  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details EDC, first 6 and last 4 digits, cardholder name, and expiry. The cashier will be able to delete the payments received in case the customer wants to pay through another payment mode.
  • Cheque Payment Mode:
  • The cashier can opt for cheque payment mode in case the customer wants to pay through a cheque. Here, by default, the amount to be received or the balance amount will be shown on the received amount field, and the cashier can enter the cheque number, bank, and date of the cheque given by the customer.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details like cheque number, bank name, and date of the cheque.
  • Foreign Currency Payment Mode:

    • The cashier can opt for foreign payment mode in case the customer wants to pay in different currency other than the store currency. Here, the cashier can enter the amount received from the customer in foreign currency and select the currency given by the customer.
    • On selecting the currency, amount in local currency will be shown which converts the amount received in foreign currency into local currency of the store based on the currency rate configured in currency master. Similarly the Balance amount in local currency will display the balance amount left in local currency.
    • For example : If the bill amount is 2974.00 and store currency is INR, iif the customer pays 30$ and currency is selected as USD then the amount in local currency will display INR 2445.00 and balance amount in local currency will be 529.00.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details like currency, Amount in local currency and the amount paid in foreign currency.
  • Demand Draft Payment Mode:

    • The cashier can opt for demand draft payment mode in case the customer wants to pay via demand draft(DD). Here, by default, the amount to be received or the balance amount will be shown on the received amount field and the cashier can enter the DD number, bank, and date of the Demand Draft received from the customer.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details like Demand draft number, bank name, date of the Demand Draft.
  • Traveller's Cheque Payment Mode:

    • The cashier can opt for Traveller's cheque payment mode in case the customer wants to pay via Traveller's cheque.  Here, the cashier can enter the amount received from the customer via traveller's cheque and select the bank, date of the cheque and currency given by the customer.
    • On selecting the currency, amount in local currency will be shown which converts the amount received in foreign currency into local currency of the store based on the currency rate configured in currency master. Similarly Balance amount in local currency will display the balance amount left in local currency.
    • For example: If the bill amount is 2974.00 and store currency is INR, iif the customer pays 30$ and currency is selected as USD then the amount in local currency will display INR 2445.00 and balance amount in local currency will be 529.00.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details like traveler’s cheque number, bank name, cheque date, currency, amount in local currency and the amount paid in foreign currency.
  • Bank Transfer Payment Mode: 

    • The cashier can opt for bank transfer payment mode in case the customer wants to pay online by transferring the amount to store’s bank account. Here, by default, the amount to be received or the balance amount will be shown on the received amount field and the cashier can select the retailers bank account, enter the customer account number, customer name, IFSC code, customer bank name, and transaction reference number.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details like Customer name, customer account number, customer bank name and transaction reference number.
  • Gift Voucher Payment Mode:
  • The cashier can opt for gift voucher payment mode in case the customer wants to redeem the gift voucher available with the customer. Here, the cashier can scan/enter the serial number of the gift voucher and if the scanned gift voucher is valid then the details of the gift voucher like GV Code, Expiry, GV amount will be displayed in the panel below.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details like Gift voucher code, expiry date of the gift voucher and the amount of the gift voucher redeemed by the customer.
  • Credit Note Payment Mode:
  • The cashier can opt for credit note payment mode in case the customer wants to redeem the credit note  available with the customer. Here, the cashier can scan/enter the credit note number and if the scanned credit note is valid then the details of the credit note like credit note number, expiry date of the credit note, available amount in the credit note and the balance amount of the credit note will be displayed.

     
  • Here the customers can redeem the credit note partially if the property Allow Excess Credit Note is enabled for the store and for the remaining partial amount a new credit note will be generated which can be redeemed by the customer later on.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details like Gift voucher code, expiry date of the gift voucher, and the amount of the gift voucher redeemed by the customer.
  • E-Wallet Payment Modes: 

    • The cashier can opt for E-wallet payment modes in case the customer wants to pay online using available E-wallets (Eg- PayTm, PhonePe, Gpay, etc). Here, by default, the amount to be received or the balance amount will be shown on the received amount field and the cashier can select the EDC device being used to process the Ewallet payment and the transaction reference number of the payment.
  • Upon accepting the payment, the received payment will be shown in the below grid along with the details like name of the ewallet, EDC device of the Ewallet, transaction reference number of the payment received.

Action Buttons:

 

  • Reprint: 
    • The cashier can use the reprint button to access the previously generated invoices, filter out the required invoice and print the invoice in either duplicate version or the original version. The cashier can also specify the number of copies required for reprinting the existing invoice.

       
    • On clicking on the reprint action button a popup window will appear displaying the previously generated invoices of the store where the cashier can filter out the required invoices by using the searchable criterias like invoice number, customer name or mobile number, From Date and To Date of the invoices generated, type of the invoice(Bill, Order, Return).
  • The cashier can also preview the invoices, and send the invoice to the respective customer via Email, Whatsapp, or SMS using the options available in the 3dots against each invoice. Customers can also receive digital receipts which provide additional information like details of the products and promotions available with the invoices.
  • Hold/UnHold:
  • The cashier can hold the bill after the products are scanned in the grid by clicking on the hold action button. On clicking a popup message will be shown whether to hold the bill and on clicking Yes a hold voucher number will be generated and the cashier can also print the hold voucher if required.
  • After the hold voucher is generated, the cashier can unhold the voucher by clicking on the unhold button which will open up the unhold window displaying all the hold voucher of the logged in counter.

     
  • In the unhold window, user can filter the unhold voucher by using searchable criterias like hold voucher number, date of the voucher, customer name and the counter if the cashier wants to unhold the hold vouchers of another counter using the toggle button (All counters) available on the top right of the unhold voucher window.
  • Scan To remove products:
  • This button can be used to remove the scanned products from the product grid in case the customer does not want those products in the bill.
  • On clicking this button, scan to remove products mode will be enabled and the cashier can rescan the products which will remove the products from the grid. In case there is more than one quantity available for the product then the product will be moved to the top of the grid.
  • Gift Voucher Mode:
    • The Cashier can also sell gift vouchers (both physical and digital) to the customers using the gift voucher mode available on the right side of the screen.

       
    • On clicking this button gift voucher mode will be enabled and the cashier will be able to scan only the gift vouchers in the product search box on saving the bill gift voucher format will be generated along with the invoice.
