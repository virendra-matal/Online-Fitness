Bank Deposit/Withdrawal

The objective of the Bank deposit and withdrawal function is set up for different purposes like for doing withdrawal and  deposit. Deposits and withdrawal can also be made through this module.

Bank deposit is a fundamental financial transaction where you entrust your money to a bank, allowing you to access various banking services while keeping your funds safe and potentially earning interest on them.

For example,Sarah receives her monthly paycheck of 2,000. She decides to deposit this money into her savings account at a local bank.This money is now safe and available for her to use whenever she needs it, and she may also earn interest on the deposited amount over time.

Creating a new single record -The user needs to go to the plus icon on the list screen of banking deposit  and click Create. After clicking on create the user will see two options i.e deposit and withdrawal. User has to select on deposit and a new page for creating a new record will appear

Search by from Node- The ETP Unify user can search from the node in the search tab  (searchable dropdown )from the amount received from the  node which they want to deposit money.  Node can be created from fulfilment node. 

Date (Calendar)- The ETP Unify user can select the date when he would like to deposit money and then click on the apply option. The date of a deposit in a bank is the day on which the funds are transferred into the bank account.

Bank (Dropdown)-  Users can select different banks created for deposit purposes  in Banking Master. Name of the banks can be created in the common master and the created options of the bank will be shown in the dropdown. Users can select the bank which they want to deposit in . 

Account (Dropdown)- Users can select respective accounts as per the Banks filtered based on transaction type account type can be created in the common master and the created options of account will be shown in the dropdown. Users can select the account which they want to deposit in .Account can be selected on the basis of type of account.

Collection Agency (Dropdown)- Users can select their respective collection agency based on the node selected.Collection Agency e can be created in the common master and the created options of .account will be shown in the dropdown. Users can select the Collection Agency which they want to deposit in . This will be a non-mandatory field. A collection agency is an organization that helps  in collecting overdue debts on behalf of creditors.
